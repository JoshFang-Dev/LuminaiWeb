Our Richmond Showroom

Who We Are

Founded in Richmond, BC, Luminai Auto Group started with a simple mission: make buying a premium pre-owned vehicle a genuinely enjoyable experience. No pressure tactics, no hidden fees, just honest cars at fair prices.

Our showroom on No. 3 Road in Richmond is home to a carefully curated selection of vehicles spanning luxury sedans, SUVs, sports cars, and everyday drivers — all hand-picked and inspected by our expert team.

We believe every customer deserves dealership-level quality without dealership-level games. That's the Luminai Auto difference.
